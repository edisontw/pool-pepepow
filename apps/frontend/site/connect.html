Connection checklist
  • Use a PEPEPOW wallet address as the miner username.
  • Add an optional worker suffix after a dot, such as YOUR_WALLET.worker01.
  • Select the PEPEPOW hoohash algorithm supported by your miner.
  • Connect to pool.pepepow.net:39333.
  • Leave password as x unless your miner requires another placeholder.

Notes

  • Website: https://pool.pepepow.net
  • Use your PEPEPOW wallet address as the username.
  • Worker names can be appended with a dot suffix (e.g. YOUR_WALLET.worker01).
  • After mining starts, use Miner Lookup to confirm accepted shares for your wallet.
  • This pool is a community-operated reference implementation. Accepted shares are tracked pool-side, and recorded payments are published after review or private self-test completion.

Security boundary

The public website is for miner connection help, pool snapshots, and recorded payment history. It does not expose operator admin tools.
